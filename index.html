About Tim

After spending 14 years leading small companies in the retail industry Tim has decided to get back to his roots of being in the tech feild. He is a graduate of ITT Technical Institute, where he graduated top of his class with an Associates of Applied Science in Electronics Engineering Technology in 1998. He also takes pride in the fact that he became a member of the National Technical Honors Society because of this.

Tim and His wife (Erin) just recently moved to Durham, NC where they plan to grow their family, adding to the two beautiful Pitbulls that they already have. He is an avid outdoorsman and former college athlete and he tries his hardest to get outside anytime he can. He supports local businesses, teams, and universities. He loves good food, dark beer and the company of great people.
